Guidebook

Boohbah: The Last Hope

Real life combat against the dangerous boohbahs and their minions turned card game!
Battle against those hypnotized by the boohbahs and progress this adventure to destroying them.
Build parties of a select few students unaffected by the hypnotization and fight through the enemies.

Game Flow

Each match will proceed as follows:
Create your party -> Prepare To Duel -> Roll Phase -> Action Phase -> End Phase


Create your party
  • Select the 3 characters you will use to defeat the enemy.
  • Enemies have advantages and disadvantages to character attributes and damage types.
  • Select your characters wisely to defeat the enemy easily.

Prepare to Duel
  • Select one of your characters to be the active character.

Roll Phase
  • During the roll phase, you will roll 10 dice.
  • After throwing the dice, you have 1 chance to choose any dice and reroll them.
  • Each dice have the following character attributes: Attack, Support, Tank
  • Characters of the same attribute can use that dice.

Action Phase
  • Each side take turns to take tacion, starting from the player who goes first.
  • See the section "Taking Action" for further information.

End Phase
  • When both players decalre end of their round, end phase begins.
  • Various card effects triggered by "End Phase" will start.
  • The next round begins.

Taking Action

During the Action Phase, after completing 1 action, you will pass your turn to the opponent.

Actions include:

Using Character Moves
  • Spend the relevant participation points based on their role.
  • Using a normal attack or skill will gain 1 energy.
  • Once a character maxes out on energy, they can spend all of it to use their utlimate.

Switching Character
  • Spend 1 participation point to switch one of your characters on stanby to be the active character.

Declaring Round End
  • Give up on making further actions this round.
  • The player who ends first will go first next round.
  • After a player declares round end, the other player will continue to take actions.
  • Once both players made their declaration, the action phase will end and the end phase will begin.

Character Roles

Each character plays a different role in how the game plays. Each character can have up to two roles.


Attack
  • Damage Dealer
  • Either deals dmg on or off-field

Support
  • Buffs damage
  • Debuffs enemies
  • Heal allies

Tank
  • Damage reduction
  • Shields

Damage Types

Each damaging move a character has falls under these 3 categories. Each type excels in different combat scenarios

Single-Target
Excels in dealing high amounts of damage to a single target.

Damage Over Time
Little in damage, its effects will last long until the enemy is defeated.

Area of Effect
Deals damage to multiple enemies at once.

Attributes

Each enemy has an attribute bar that increases when characters of the same attribute attack. Each attack has differing levels of attribute stacks given. Enemies can have higher/lower requirements in the amount of stacks to proc the attribute effect.

Coder
Accumulate debug stacks and stun the enemy for 2 rounds. Damage during stun is increased by 1.

Cyber
Accumulate network security stacks and summon a firewall, lasting 2 rounds.
Firewall:

  • Reduce damage taken by 1
  • Counterattack, dmg calculated based on 1/2 of the initial attack damage + 1 damage.

Biotech
Accumulate chemical agents and summon poison, damaging enemies and healing the party based on poison damage.
Poison:

  • Deal 2 damage at the end of the round. (5 rounds)
  • Heal 1/2 of poison damage

Other Rules

Damage Calculation
  1. The present player's card effects will be resolved first before the card effects of the enemy.
  2. The card effects belonging to the same player will be resolved in the following order:
    1. Character Card
    2. Character Statuses
    3. Team Combat Status
    4. Summons

Piercing Damage
  • These attacks can't be buffed or nerfed, but shields and invulnerabilities of the opponent are ignored.

Duration(Rounds)
  • Some effects have limited duration.
  • Each End Phase, 1 round will be removed from such an effect's duration.
  • Once duraction runs out, it will be discarded.

Usages
  • Some effects have limited usages.
  • Each time this effect is triggered, 1 usage will be consumed.
  • Once usage runs out, it will be discarded.
